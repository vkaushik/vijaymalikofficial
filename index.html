    About Vijay

    Intro

    Vijay Malik born on April 5 1989 is not only a naturally gifted vocalist; he has the ability to attract legions of admirers to his concerts. He has been evolving rapidly & incessantly. It was during his graduation that this music-loving soul decided to live & breathe music. While representing his college in various techno & youth fests,the exceptional, unexampled, exclusive & alluring quality in his rendering of classical as well as pop music fascinated audience & he finished many a competitions with distinction. He has shown his versatility by covering a wide variety of singers & people commend his modus operandi. His vigorous & ardent attitude towards music attracted the Hindi newspaper, Dainik Jagran's team, Vijay Malik was nominated for its show, "Hayana ke No.1 Singer"(2011) & he won the title.
